Warehouse Technician

Location: Ottawa

A market leader in the design and retail of mid- to high-end furniture for interiors and outdoors, HOME SOCIETE is a division of G2MC, a family-owned Canadian company with a portfolio of renowned brands including Maison Corbeil, MUST and Jardin de Ville. As a follow-up to the resounding success of our first store in Toronto, we are pleased to announce the opening of a new location in Ottawa, at 2685 Iris Street.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Working with merchandising & operations team to maintain organization within the warehouse & on the sales floor;
  • Follow the procedures for inventory movements (customer pick-ups, pick-up for delivery, loading and transfers between stores);
  • Load, unload, move, wrap, assemble and place products on the sales floor;
  • Provide excellent customer service;
  • Work with the inventory management computer system;
  • Respect the health and safety rules.

DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • A positive attitude and ability to work well in a team setting;
  • Problem solving skills;
  • The knowledge to neatly and professionally assemble, disassemble and wrap high end furniture and accessories;
  • Good physical shape;
  • Be organized and prioritize work as necessary;
  • Detail-oriented;
  • Ability to work with minimal supervision;
  • Bilingualism (an asset).

SOME OF WHAT WE OFFER

  • A permanent full-time and/or part-time position;
  • Competitive salary according to experience;
  • A complete and paid training program;
  • Long-term career opportunities.
